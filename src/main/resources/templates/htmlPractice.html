TRAVEL

Traveling has always been one of the most meaningful ways for me to explore the world, experience new cultures, and grow as a person. I’ve been fortunate enough to visit a variety of incredible destinations across Asia, North America, and Europe, each leaving a unique impression on me. In Asia, I’ve wandered the vibrant streets of Hong Kong, felt the dynamic energy of Bangkok in Thailand, and experienced both the modern and traditional sides of Tokyo, Osaka, and the beautiful Okinawa in Japan. I’ve explored the lively night markets and warm hospitality of Taipei in Taiwan, and discovered the impressive blend of history and innovation in Shanghai, China. My journeys have also taken me to Toronto, Canada, where I admired the city's multicultural spirit, and to the iconic New York City, where I was inspired by its endless possibilities and unforgettable skyline. In Europe, I’ve walked through the romantic streets of Paris, stood in awe of the timeless beauty of Rome, and experienced the vibrant culture and history of London. Each city taught me something new — about people, about the world, and about myself. These adventures continue to shape who I am today, fueling my curiosity and inspiring me to keep exploring. Every destination has a story, and I look forward to writing many more.
